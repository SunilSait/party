FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for standard packages and 4-6 weeks for custom or large-scale events. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee.

Do you provide setup and cleanup?

Yes! All our service packages include professional setup and post-event cleanup. Our team arrives 2-4 hours before your event to ensure everything is picture-perfect.

Can I customize the colors and theme?

Absolutely! Every package can be customized to match your color palette, theme, and personal style. We offer free color consultations with all bookings.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater metro area and surrounding suburbs within a 50-mile radius. For events beyond this range, delivery fees may apply. Contact us for details.

Do you offer bulk order discounts?

Yes! We offer volume discounts for bulk orders and recurring events. Corporate clients receive special pricing tiers. Check the bulk pricing table above for details.
