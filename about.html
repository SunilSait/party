Who We Are

Bringing Joy to Every Celebration

Founded in 2012, PartyPop started as a small balloon art studio with a big dream — to make every celebration feel magical. What began as a passion project for two childhood friends has grown into a full-service party decoration company trusted by thousands.

Today, we offer everything from intimate birthday setups to grand wedding installations, serving clients across the country with the same creative passion and attention to detail that started it all.
