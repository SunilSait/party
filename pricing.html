FAQ

Pricing FAQ

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. A 50% deposit is required at booking, with the balance due 3 days before the event.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 14 days before the event for a full refund. Cancellations within 7-14 days receive a 50% refund. Less than 7 days notice is non-refundable but transferable to a new date.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely! All packages are fully customizable. You can mix and match elements, adjust quantities, and add unique touches. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific needs.

Are there additional charges for delivery?

Delivery and setup are included within a 15-mile radius. For locations beyond this area, a small delivery fee may apply. Contact us with your venue address for an exact quote.
